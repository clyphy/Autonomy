## 📊 THE ESSENTIAL METRICS

You don't need to track everything, but these four metrics tell you if your creative work is healthy:

### **T_I: Temporal Integrity** [0-1]
**Question:** "Is this consistent with what I've created before?"
- 0.9+: Excellent coherence
- 0.7-0.9: Good, minor inconsistencies
- <0.7: Major continuity problems, needs revision

### **E_R: Emotional Resonance** [0-1]
**Question:** "Does this make me feel something real?"
- 0.9+: Deeply moving
- 0.7-0.9: Emotionally engaging

### **S_T: Sovereign Trust** [0-∞]
**Question:** "Am I getting more out than I'm putting in?"
- >1.0: Generative (you're in the flow)
- =1.0: Balanced
- <1.0: Extractive (you're depleting yourself)

### **L_S: Love Supremacy** [0-1]
**Question:** "Is this serving love/beauty/growth?"
- 0.9+: Aligned with highest purpose
- 0.7-0.9: Generally positive direction
- <0.7: Ego-driven, recalibrate intentions

**How to measure:** These are subjective feelings, not objective numbers. After each creative session, simply ask yourself these questions and estimate. Track the patterns over time.
